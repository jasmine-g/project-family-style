How To play

  • After creating or joining a room,
    the Headchef (person who created the room)
    positions the players,this will be important later in the game.
  • At the top of the screen, it shows you the dish you need to serve.
    Following the given dish, place the ingredients on the plate and swipe up to serve!
  • If you do not have a certain ingredient, one your friends probably has it!
    To pass an ingredient, simply slide the ingredient to your left or right.
    The player who recieves it depends on who the headchef positioned on your left and right.
  • Every round is a race against time!
    Sadly, if a certain amount of dishes is not completed in the set time frame,
    your group of friends will not make it to the next round.
    New ingredients, kitchen tools and dishes will be introduced as you pass the rounds!
